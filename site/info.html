Information for Computer Science Students

Useful Sites

  • BlackBoard. You will use this site mostly to view information on the course. Each course has a separate page where you can also find useful links to for example the course book.
  • MyTimetable. On this site, you can view your time table for the courses you are taking.
  • SV Sticky. SV Sticky is the study association of the Computer Science bachelor here in Utrecht. There, you can get a cup of coffee or tea, and offer books for courses at discounted prices.
  • Remindo. This site is used for online examinations and tests. Most courses will have a final exam that uses this platform.
  • Osiris Student. You will be registering for courses on this site. Your final grades for each course are also visible on Osiris.
  • Bachelor Informatica Page. This site provides information on the bachelor.

Practical Information

You Have Rights!

The OER is there to protect the rights of both the students and teachers. This document states, for example, how long teachers have to grade your exams and practicals. If a teacher does something that is unacceptable, check the OER! Maybe that teacher is doing something that is not allowed.

  • OER (English)
